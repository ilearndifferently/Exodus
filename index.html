Architectural Overview

Exodus is not just a collection of stories; it is highly structured. It tells the epic story of God rescuing the Israelites from Egyptian slavery (Liberation), establishing a covenant relationship with them at Mount Sinai (Law), and instructing them to build a Tabernacle so His presence can dwell among them (Presence).

Ch 1-18 Deliverance from Egypt
Ch 19-24 Covenant & Law
Ch 25-40 The Tabernacle
